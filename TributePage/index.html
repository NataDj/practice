Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo y Calderón

“ I have never expected anything from my work but the satisfaction I could get from it by the very fact of painting and saying what I couldn’t say otherwise.”

Here's a time line of Frida's life:

  • 1907 - born in Coyoacán, a village on the outskirts of Mexico City
  • 1925 - suffered bus accident and was severy injured.
  • 1928 - Kahlo was introduced to Diego Rivera
  • 1930 - Kahlo and Rivera moved to U.S.A.
  • 1934 - Kahlo and Rivera moved back to Mexico
  • 1950 - Kahlo spent most of the year in Hospital ABC in Mexico City
  • 1953 - She became severely depressed and anxious, and her dependency on painkillers escalated
  • 1954 - On the night of 12 July 1954, Kahlo had a high fever and was in extreme pain. At approximately 6 a.m. on 13 July 1954, her nurse found her dead in her bed. Kahlo was 47 years old
